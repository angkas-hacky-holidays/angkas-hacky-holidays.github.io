View Mechanics

Join us for an exhilarating 24-hour hackathon where creativity and innovation take center stage! This event is open to our internal tech team and their friends. Bring your ideas to life and compete for amazing prizes!

Event Details

  • Date: December 12-13, 2024
  • Venue: Angkas HQ and Virtual
    In-Person: Angkas HQ, Sabio Street, Makati City
    Virtual: A link for online participation will be shared with all registered participants.

Schedule Overview

Day 1 (December 12)

  • 12:00 PM: Welcome and Kickoff
  • 12:15 PM: Hacking Begins
  • Mentor Sessions available throughout the event

Day 2 (December 13)

  • 12:15 PM: Submission Deadline
  • 1:30 PM: Final Presentations
  • 4:30 PM: Judging and Award Ceremony

Who Can Participate

  • Internal Tech & Product Team: All team members are welcome!
  • Friends: Invite your friends! Whether they‚Äôre tech enthusiasts or just curious, we welcome participants from all backgrounds.

Team Rules

  • Teams must consist of 2-3 members.
  • Non-tech individuals are encouraged to join for diverse perspectives.
  • Registration closes 24 hours before the event.

Hackathon Rules

  • All projects must be original and created during the hackathon.
  • Participants can use any tech stack or tools they prefer.
  • Each team will present their project for 5 minutes during final presentations.
  • Teams must provide a GitHub repository or equivalent with code and documentation for judges to review.
  • Participants retain ownership of their projects.

Judging Criteria

  • Technical Innovation: How original and cutting-edge is the technology?
  • Creativity: Does the solution demonstrate outside-the-box thinking?
  • User Experience: Is the project user-friendly and engaging?
  • Overall Impact: Does the project solve a real-world problem effectively?

Food and Refreshments

Meals and refreshments will be provided throughout the event. Please inform us in advance about any dietary restrictions.

Prizes

  • Grand Prize: Keyboard and Hacky Holidays T-shirt
  • 2nd Runner-Up: Hacky Holidays T-shirt
  • Crowd Favorite: Hacky Holidays T-shirt
